Temporal Disparity Illusion

Reveals hidden flows of motion by transforming mathematical sequences and geometric arrangements into visual patterns. Using the CIELAB color space, color and lightness become precise tools to sculpt perception.

Pumpkin Illusion

Shows how parametric variations in color, lightness, and contour geometry generate emergent motion within a static image. By embedding bicolor patterns into Kusama’s iconic pumpkin and synchronizing them with oscillating background phases, the work demonstrates a perceptual transformation: from fixed form to dynamic flow.
